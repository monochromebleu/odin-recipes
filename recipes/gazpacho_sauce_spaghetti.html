Gazpacho Sauce Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Ready in just 15 minutes this simple vegan pasta recipe is tossed in a smooth yet fiery gazpacho sauce

Ingredients

  • 160g spaghetti
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • ½ a clove garlic
  • ½ red onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
  • a good dash Tabasco
  • a small bunch basil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, put the tomatoes, pepper, garlic and onion into a blender and whizz until completely smooth
  3. Season, then stir in the sherry vinegar and Tabasco.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain well and toss with the sauce. Tear in lots of basil and serve.
