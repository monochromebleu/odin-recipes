Red Lentil Lasagna

Description

This easy vegan lasagna is gluten free, nut free and packed with plant protein. Great if you're looking for a healthy weeknight dinner with portions to spare!

Ingredients

Filling

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 large zucchini/courgette, chopped into 1cm cubes (about 2 cups)
  • 1 cup uncooked red lentils
  • 6 (approx.) gluten free or regular lasagna sheets/noodles as needed
  • 2 tbsp dried mixed Italian herbs
  • 1 cup vegan cheese shreds or slices
  • 3 cups crushed tomatoes (just under one 800ml can)
  • 1½ cup vegetable stock (1 stock cube dissolved in 1½ cups boiling water))
  • 1 large red bell pepper, chopped into 1cm cubes (about 1½ cups)

Cheese Sauce

  • 2 cups unsweetened soy or coconut milk
  • ¼ cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder

Steps

For the Sauce

  1. In a medium saucepan on a low heat, combine all sauce ingredients (using a metal whisk to remove any lumps if needed).
  2. Heat the mixture for about 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until it thickens and resembles cheese sauce.
  3. Remove from the heat and set aside.

For the Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F/175°C.
  2. Add garlic, zucchini and red pepper and sauté for a further 5 minutes, continuing to stir.
  3. Pour in the tomatoes, lentils, stock and dried herbs. Increase the heat to bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and cover with a lid.
  4. Simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the lentils are completely soft.

To Assemble

  1. Pour the tomato/lentil filling into a deep ovenproof dish (just enough to cover the bottom of the dish).
  2. Cover this with 3 of the lasagna sheets/noodles, and pour half the cheese sauce over these evenly. (If your cheese sauce has become too thick while you've been making the tomato/lentil filling, simply reheat it very gently or thin it out with a small amount of water.)
  3. Repeat the process, using all the remaining tomato/lentil filling, the remaining 3 lasagna sheets and the remaining cheese sauce.
  4. Sprinkle cheese shreds over the top, and cover the dish in foil.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes in the oven, remove the foil and bake for a further 15 minutes until cheese/cheese sauce is golden.
  6. Serve with your favourite salad for a hearty, protein-packed dinner!
